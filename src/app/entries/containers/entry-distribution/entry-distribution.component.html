Comments

The additional voluntary contribution will have to be passed on to the workers. Since in kind benefits and bonuses can be an important component of remuneration, it is important to include them in the calculation. In this section you can specify what % of the voluntary contribution you want to allocate to the respective category.

Please be advised that based on the principles for in-kind benefits outlined in the Anker Methodology, contribution of in-kind benefits (i.e. non-monetary benefits such as food, transport or housing provided by the employer that reduce the amount of cash income that workers need for a decent standard of living) to total renumeration is limited to ensure the worker right to self determination. The combined total value of in-kind benefits is capped at 30% of total remuneration. Housing is capped at 15% of total remuneration. All other benefits are capped at 10% of total remuneration. In order to reflect realities on the ground for now you are able to exceed the maximum specified. However this will have implications on the total renumeration calculated in the IDH salary matrix.
